Burritos

Description

Our go to meal for weeknight dinners

Ingredients

Burritos

  • 4 Boneless Skinless Chicken Thighs
  • Marinade
  • 1 can Black Beans
  • 2 cups Mexican or Spanish rice
  • Shredded Cheese

Merinade

  • 1/2 cup Soy Sauce
  • 1/2 cup Avocado Oil
  • 1 tablespoon Paprika
  • 1 tablespoon Cumin
  • 1 tablespoon Oregano

Steps

  1. Prepare the Marinade
  2. Cut the chicken into 1/2 inch pieces
  3. Marinade Chicken for at least 30 minutes
  4. Prepare rice per packaging
  5. Warm beans in a saucepan
  6. Cook chicken in a skillet until cooked through
  7. Prepare burritos and top with whatever you want.
  8. Enjoy!

Marinade

Whisk all marinade ingredients together and marinade chicken for at least 30 minutes
