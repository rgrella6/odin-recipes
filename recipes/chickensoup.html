Lemon Meringue Pie

Description

I make this soup any time someone in the house is sick. Always helps them feel better

Ingredients

  • 4 Chicken Thighs
  • 2 quarts Chicken Stock
  • 1 cube chicken boullion
  • 3 carrots diced
  • 3 celery ribs diced
  • 1 onion diced
  • 1 teaspoon Rosemary, Thyme and Basil
  • 1 package egg noodles

Steps

  1. In a large stock pot, sear the chicken thighs on high heat, then remove from the pot
  2. Add the carrots, celery and onion to the pot, Cook for 4-5 minutes
  3. Add the stock to the pot along with the boillion cube and seasonings. Bring to a boil
  4. Add the chicken back to the pot, Cook for 10 minutes to finish cooking the chicken
  5. Remove the chicken and shred using 2 forks
  6. Add egg noodles to the pot and cook per the packaging
  7. Re-add the shredded chicken to the pot
  8. Mix then serve
